The AI Tutor

  • According to data from Glass Door, university tutors earn around $47 p/h. For example x4 tutors in our cohort
  • Opportunities:
    1. Educational institutions can reduce costs by using our service.
    2. Deliver a personalised education experience for students. Move away from cohort based education strategies.
    3. Education programs can be delivered at the students preferred pace. With extra help from the AI Tutor, every student can succeed!
    4. Create 'Machine-Learning Based Education as a Platform'. Offer significant advantages over existing clunky LMS's like Canvas
    5. Third party education providers pay to use our platform as a LMS
